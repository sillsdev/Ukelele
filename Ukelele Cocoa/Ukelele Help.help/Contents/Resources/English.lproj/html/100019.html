You can drag and drop a character, or even a string of up to 20 Unicode characters, onto a key in the keyboad layout window to change its output. The modifiers that are active at the time you drop the character (or string of characters) onto the key are those that come into play.

Where do you get characters? Virtually any application that handles text will let you drag text out of its window and into Ukeleleâ€™s keyboard layout window. Otherwise, the Character Viewer can be a good source. You can access this from the input menu (the one with a flag), or from the Emoji & Symbols command in the Edit menu.
