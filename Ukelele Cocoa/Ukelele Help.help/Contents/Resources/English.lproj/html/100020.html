Another method of editing output is to double-click the key. When you do so, a popover appears with the current output selected, and you just need to enter the new output and click Done.

If the key is a special key (e.g. escape, tab, return, arrow key, F-key), then a button is enabled for you to reset it to the standard output for that key.
